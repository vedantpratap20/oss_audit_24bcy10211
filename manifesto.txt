Open Source Manifesto
Author: Vedant Pratap Singh
Date: 29 Mar 2026
------------------------

I rely on VLC Media Player on a daily basis. It was built by folks who chose to share their code instead of hiding it.

To me, freedom is collaboration. Software freedom allows anyone to learn and improve tools.

If I could build something to give back, it would be a free educational platform.
Sharing knowledge is how we build a better world.
